# Reflection

Building this portfolio has helped me see more clearly how my ideas develop through reading, writing and revision. It has shown me that a strong argument rarely appears fully formed; it usually becomes sharper through comparing evidence, questioning assumptions and returning to earlier drafts.

The process has also clarified the areas I want to explore further, particularly the relationship between economics, business, public policy and Asia. As the portfolio grows, I want to use it not only to present finished work, but also to track how my judgement, confidence and independence develop over time.
